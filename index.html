Recipe Of The Day: Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorno cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with greated cheeses.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix the mixture.
  5. Remove from heat and stir the mixture.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve imedeiatly.

Nutrient Amount Per Serving
Calories 500
